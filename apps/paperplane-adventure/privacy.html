Privacy Policy for Paper Plane Adventure

Effective date: April 25, 2026
App: Paper Plane Adventure (com.jarissapps.paperplane)
Developer: JarissApps

This Privacy Policy describes how Paper Plane Adventure ("the app", "we", "our") handles information when you use the app. We are committed to keeping your data minimal and your experience private.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) about you. The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide your name, email, phone number, or location.

The only information stored by the app is:

  • Your high score, saved locally on your device using your browser/system's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear app storage.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service, which may collect information independently as described in its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID), general location (approximate, derived from IP), and ad interaction data to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can review Google's privacy practices and opt out of personalized ads here:
    • Google Privacy Policy
    • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

3. Permissions Used

The app may request the following device permissions, only when needed for a specific feature:

  • Internet access — required to load advertisements via AdMob.
  • Storage / Files — used only when you choose to share a screenshot of your score; the image is written temporarily so the system share sheet can attach it. No files are uploaded by the app itself.

4. Sharing Feature

When you tap "Share" inside the app, the app uses your device's native share sheet to let you send a score image to an app of your choosing (e.g., Messages, Mail, social apps). The app itself does not transmit, upload, or retain a copy of what you share. Whatever you choose to share is governed by the privacy policy of the destination app.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service used by the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action with that service provider.

6. Data Security

Because the app does not transmit personal data to our servers (we do not operate any), there is no remote data store to breach. Locally stored data (your high score) is protected by your device's normal app sandboxing.

7. Your Choices

  • Reset local data: uninstalling the app or clearing its storage in Android Settings will erase your local high score.
  • Limit ad personalization: on Android, go to Settings → Privacy → Ads to reset or opt out of your Advertising ID.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Effective date" above. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
